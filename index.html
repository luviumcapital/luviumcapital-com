Our Story

Luvium Capital was founded on the principle that every viable business deserves access to growth capital. We recognized a critical gap in the South African market: thousands of vehicle dealerships in rural and underserved areas unable to access the floor plan financing they need to thrive.

Our innovative approach combines institutional-grade risk management with a deep understanding of local market dynamics, enabling us to serve dealerships that traditional lenders overlook.

Today, we're proud to be driving financial inclusion while delivering attractive risk-adjusted returns to our investors.

Our Solution

A comprehensive floor plan financing solution tailored for underserved vehicle dealerships

The Challenge

South Africa's used vehicle market is valued at over R20 billion annually, yet thousands of legitimate dealerships in rural and underserved areas lack access to floor plan financing. Traditional lenders focus on established urban dealers, leaving a significant market segment without the capital needed to stock inventory and grow their businesses.

This creates a lose-lose situation: dealerships cannot reach their potential, communities lack access to quality vehicles, and investors miss out on attractive risk-adjusted returns in an underserved market.

How It Works

1

Fund

Luvium provides R3.5M in floor plan financing to qualified dealerships, enabling them to purchase and stock quality used vehicles.

2

Grow

Dealerships use the capital to expand their inventory, increase sales, and grow their businesses while serving their communities.

3

Repay

As vehicles are sold, dealerships repay the financing with interest, generating consistent returns for investors.
